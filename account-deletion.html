Account & Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Aiko account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at stevebaconpants@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Aiko account.
  2. Include the subject line: “Aiko Account Deletion Request”

What data is deleted

  • User account information (email, user ID)
  • User-generated content (images, posts)

What data may be retained

  • Legal or security logs retained for up to 30 days

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
